# Cail Smith

"People on the East Coast are resilient, but they should not have to be resilient just to get by. I learned resilience early because I had to. It shaped me, but it also showed me that getting by should not require constant struggle."

## About
[Cail Smith](http://www.linkedin.com/in/cail-smith-06ba20317) is Opportunity's candidate for East Cape. Living and working in Gisborne, he leads people and services within the region, giving him firsthand insight into the pressures local families face and the operational challenges that come with living in an isolated, weather‑affected part of the country. Through hard work, he has built his reputation as a practical problem‑solver – someone who understands systems, the people within them, and how to make them work better.

With strong ties across the community through volunteering, sport, and family, Cail has a grounded view of how stretched local infrastructure, housing, and support services have become, and how much potential the region has when communities are backed with the right planning and investment.

Cail is standing to bring practical leadership, community insight, and long‑term thinking to the East Cape – with a commitment to championing solutions that strengthen the region and create real opportunities for the people who call it home.
