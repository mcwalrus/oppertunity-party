## About
Cody has served the Northcote community since 2015 as a Paramedic — and now as your local voice for Opportunity.

In addition to extensive clinical experience in emergency services, hospitals and clinics, he runs a business building foilboards. This gives Cody a thorough understanding of the challenges facing small business ownership. These experiences have made him passionate about improving access to core services such as healthcare and emergency services, addressing key transportation infrastructure, and delivering real solutions that will make living in New Zealand affordable for future generations.

Cody moved from Gisborne to Tāmaki Makaurau Auckland in 2012 to attend Auckland University of Technology. This was when he fell in love with the North Shore beaches and vibrant community. Cody lives in Hillcrest with his partner and their dog Obi, and is motivated to create a better future for all New Zealanders.

Cody looks forward to continuing to serve the people of Northcote electorate as their Opportunity candidate.
