# Healthy Land

**We depend on nature, but we're stuck in old economic models that ignore the health of the ecosystems we rely on.**

At Opportunity we see a future where purpose-led businesses grow
wealth by restoring nature. But we're not there yet. In some places,
nature needs us to take a strong stand today, to protect what's left.

When half of our rivers are unswimmable and our unique biodiversity
is at risk — it's time to set bottom-lines, as well as invest in the
nature-positive economy of the future. That's why Opportunity will:

* Increase conservation and biosecurity funding — with a particular
  focus on wetlands, native reforestation and other biodiversity-rich
  projects.
* Improve water quality through strong regulation and independent
  oversight.
* Support landowners to transition to high-value, low impact land uses
  through spatial planning, nature-positive landscape management
  practices and innovative technology that reduces pesticide and
  artificial fertiliser use.
* Invest in breakthrough sectors like continuous cover native forestry
  and a nature market, where actions to improve biodiversity are
  rewarded.
* Design a circular plastics economy aligned to the objectives of the
  [New Plastics Economy Roadmap](https://www.scionresearch.com/__data/assets/pdf_file/0003/80607/MakingZeroTheHero-Summary-Report.pdf) developed by the New Zealand plastics
  industry.
