**Introducing Robert Mason - Opportunity Party Candidate for Kenepuru**

We’re delighted to welcome you to meet Robert Mason, the Opportunity Party candidate for Kenepuru.

Robert is an Oxford University graduate, a senior secondary school educator, and a seasoned political strategist. Robert has spent over fifteen years in education using evidence and data to successfully lift student achievement outcomes across diverse communities.

A Wellington local, Robert brings deep practical campaign experience to the race. He previously served as a local electorate chair, managed a successful parliamentary campaign, and stood for the Wellington City Council in 2022.

As Opportunity’s Candidate Manager and Education Policy architect, Robert is an intellectual risk-taker who champions evidence-based, collaborative decision-making.

Robert is ready to bring this unpretentious, future-focused leadership to Parliament to deliver real progress for Kenepuru.
