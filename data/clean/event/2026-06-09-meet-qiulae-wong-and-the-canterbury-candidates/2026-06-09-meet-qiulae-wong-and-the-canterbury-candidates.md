Get excited and ready to meet our candidates based right here in Canterbury.

**RSVP** **below** and **join us** at the **Rolleston Community Centre** to meet the new Opportunity party leader Qiulae Wong. In attendance will be your newly announced candidates, Adrian Mee in Selwyn and Matt Harris in Rangitata. Bolstering the line up is Christchurch Central candidate Eden Skipper. This is a great chance to meet the people standing up for your community, hear their priorities, and ask the questions that matter most to you ahead of the 2026 general election.

Come on down with a friend and make a cuppa. Take a seat and have a listen to our Canterbury team. The floor will then be opened for you to fire back questions.

**7:00pm** Doors open

**7:15pm** Welcoming address

**7:20pm** Keynote speaker Qiulae Wong followed by speeches from your local candidates Adrian, Eden, and Matt.

**8:00pm** Q&A session

For those of you that wish to become part of our volunteer team we have a range of roles that we can use your help with. This could be as simple as leaflet dropping through to phone banking, door knocking or staffing our market tent.
