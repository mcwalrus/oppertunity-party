**Meet your Opportunity Party Candidates for Hutt South & Remutaka**

Join us for an engaging **“Meet the Candidates”** event with your Opportunity Party electorate candidates for **Hutt South** and **Remutaka**! This is a great chance to meet the people standing up for your community, hear about their priorities, and ask the questions that matter most ahead of the 2026 general election.

For **Hutt South**, you’ll hear from **Dr Ben Wylie‑van-Eerd** — a career scientist passionate about looking after people in the workplace, and strengthening the New Zealand science system so that our businesses and industries can compete better on the world stage. 

Representing **Remutaka**, **Luke Ross** brings a fresh perspective rooted in his experience in the screen and broadcast industry, community storytelling, and creative communication. A Wellington local with deep ties to the Hutt Valley, Luke is passionate about **rational, evidence‑based politics** that prioritises both environmental wellbeing and local community voices at the decision‑making table.

Whether you’re curious about how the Opportunity Party plans to address housing, climate change, healthcare, or local infrastructure, this event is your opportunity to **connect with Ben and Luke, learn more about their vision, and share your ideas for the future of Hutt South and Remutaka**.

Come along, bring your questions, and help shape the conversation in our communities!
