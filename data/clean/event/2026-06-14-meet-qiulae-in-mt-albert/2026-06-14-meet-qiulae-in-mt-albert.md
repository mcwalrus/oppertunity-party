**Meet Opportunity Party Leader and Candidate for Mt Albert, Qiulae Wong!**

Qiulae is a Mt Albert local where she lives with her husband Grant and two daughters, one of whom attends Mt Albert Primary where this event will be held.

Come along to hear about the 2026 election campaign for the Opportunity Party, and in particular what this means for the Mt Albert electorate (including Pt Chevalier, Kingsland, Ōwairaka, St Lukes, Morningside, Arch Hill, Grey Lynn, and Westmere).

Qiulae will share her story, why she got into politics, why this is the year for the Opportunity Party and what their priorities are for this election.

We'd love you to come along and share your thoughts on the future of the Mt Albert electorate, where New Zealand is at and where we should be going.
